who i am

I'm Shuyang Li (李舒扬) from Shenzhen, China. I study Computer Science at Notre Dame.

I'm obsessed with technology, data science, design, and communications. I'm concerned with how we could enhance individuals and transform communities through tech and design, and I'm currently interested in the Internet of Things, wearable technology, and gamification.

I was a software engineering intern at Apple from May 2014 to August 2014.

Fun fact: OS X Yosemite shipped with a recording of my voice (reading Chinese). If you're running Yosemite, you can listen to it by running the following command:

what i do

I am research assistant for iCeNSA (Interdisciplinary Center for Network Sciences and Applications), where I worked on an Android app for a project on diabetes intervention, and am working on a project on healthcare.
