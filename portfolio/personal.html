Personal Website

The personal website is always the hardest to design. On my website, I want something minimal that visitors can interact with, and I want the interaction to be playful. Visitors will be conscious of the interaction, and understand some of my personality through playing with my website.

I built this color grid where visitors can guide the “light” with their mouse, and when the visitor stops moving, the “light,” seemingly with its own personality, will move on its own. Although the algorithm is a simple random walk, the interaction is much more interesting, even intimate to some extent.
